📘 50+ Aptitude Questions with Solutions

1. Quantitative Aptitude

Q1: A train 150 meters long is running at 60 km/h. How long will it take to cross a pole?

Solution: Speed = 60 km/h = 16.67 m/s. Time = 150 / 16.67 ≈ 9 seconds

✅ Answer: 9 seconds

Q2: The average of five numbers is 48. If one number is excluded, the average becomes 45. What is the excluded number?

Solution: Total of 5 = 5×48 = 240, Total of 4 = 4×45 = 180, Excluded = 240 - 180 = 60

✅ Answer: 60

Q3: What is the LCM of 15 and 20?

Solution: LCM = 2² × 3 × 5 = 60

✅ Answer: 60

2. Logical Reasoning

Q4: What is the next number in the series: 2, 6, 12, 20, 30, ?

Solution: Pattern = +4, +6, +8, +10 → Next = 30 + 12 = 42

✅ Answer: 42

Q5: Pointing to a man, Sara said, “He is the only son of my mother’s husband.” How is Sara related to the man?

Solution: Mother's husband = father, so Sara is sister → he is brother

✅ Answer: Brother

Q6: A person walks 5 km north, then turns right and walks 3 km, then turns right again and walks 5 km. How far is he from the starting point?

Solution: Final position is 3 km east of starting point

✅ Answer: 3 km

4. Time, Speed and Distance

Q9: A boat can travel 30 km downstream in 2 hours and upstream in 3 hours. What is the speed of the boat in still water?

Solution: Downstream = 15, Upstream = 10 → Average = (15 + 10)/2 = 12.5 km/h

✅ Answer: 12.5 km/h

5. Profit and Loss

Q10: A man buys an item for ₹500 and sells it for ₹650. What is the profit percentage?

Solution: Profit = ₹150, Profit% = (150/500) × 100 = 30%

✅ Answer: 30%

Q11: A man sells a watch for ₹720 at a loss of 10%. What was the cost price?

Solution: SP = 90% of CP → CP = 720 / 0.9 = ₹800

✅ Answer: ₹800

6. Simple and Compound Interest

Q12: What is the simple interest on ₹4000 at 5% per annum for 3 years?

Solution: SI = (P × R × T)/100 = (4000 × 5 × 3)/100 = ₹600

✅ Answer: ₹600

7. Problems on Ages

Q13: The sum of ages of A and B is 36. Four years ago, A was 4 times B’s age. What is B’s age now?

Solution: A = 26, B = 10

✅ Answer: 10 years

8. Pipes and Cisterns

Q14: Pipe A fills in 6 hrs, Pipe B empties in 8 hrs. Both open. Time to fill tank?

Solution: Net rate = 1/6 - 1/8 = 1/24 → Time = 24 hours

✅ Answer: 24 hours

9. Clocks and Calendars

Q15: What is the angle between the hour and the minute hand at 3:15?

Solution: Angle = |(30×3 + 0.5×15) - (6×15)| = |97.5 - 90| = 7.5°

✅ Answer: 7.5°

Q16: What day of the week was 1st January 2000?

Solution: Use Zeller’s Congruence → Result: Saturday

✅ Answer: Saturday

10. Probability

Q17: What is the probability of getting a prime number when a die is rolled?

Solution: Primes = 2, 3, 5 → 3 outcomes out of 6 → 3/6 = 1/2

✅ Answer: 1/2

11. Permutation and Combination

Q18: How many ways can the letters in the word "APPLE" be arranged?

Solution: 5! / 2! = 60

✅ Answer: 60 ways

12. Series and Puzzles

Q19: Find the missing number in the series: 1, 3, 6, 10, 15, ?

Solution: Pattern: +2, +3, +4, +5 → Next = 15 + 6 = 21

✅ Answer: 21

Q20: What comes next in the series: A, C, F, J, O, ?
