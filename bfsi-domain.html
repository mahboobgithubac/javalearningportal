The BFSI domain includes:

  • Banking: Retail banking, corporate banking, credit/debit card systems, core banking systems, loan management.
  • Financial Services: Asset management, wealth management, mutual funds, investment banking, stock trading platforms.
  • Insurance: Life insurance, health insurance, claims processing, policy management.

Area Role of Java Developer
